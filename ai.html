ASK YOUR DATA

Get instant answers to your business questions with AI-powered analytics.

"How many lots do I have available?"
AI analyzes your inventory database and returns the current count of available lots across all developments, with breakdowns by community and price tier.
"How many lots did I sell last month? 2 months ago?"
AI queries your sales database and provides month-over-month comparisons, highlighting trends and seasonal patterns in your sales performance.
"If I sold everything that's available, how much revenue would I generate?"
AI calculates the total potential revenue by summing the prices of all available lots, with breakdowns by development and price category.
"Which lots have been on the market the longest?"
AI identifies stale inventory and suggests pricing or marketing adjustments based on comparable sales and market conditions.
"What's my sales velocity by price point?"
AI analyzes how quickly lots sell at different price points, helping you optimize pricing strategy for maximum revenue and turnover.
"Which community has the highest profit margin?"
AI compares acquisition costs to sales prices across developments, identifying your most profitable projects and opportunities for improvement.

SEARCH LIKE YOU THINK

Buyers can now search conversationally for the perfect lot.

Your land will be discovered by AI, not buried in PDFs or sales calls.

"I am searching for a lot with north facade"
Since its a map with polygons, we know which sides of each polygon face north. If it has no colliding neighbor on its north side = facade. The lot/s get highlighted on the map.
"I am searching for a lot with the least amount of neighbors in Barcelona community"
Check which lot or lots have the least amount of colliding sides with adjacent properties.
"I am searching for a corner lot under 500k"
If a lot only has 2 or less colliding sides with other polygons, and it's not in the outer ring it's a corner lot.
"I am searching for a lot with mountain view"
Analyze 360 street-view imagery for the closest mountains. Since we have 360 imagery AI can search for "things" and together with the map vector the AI can calculate closest lot/s to the "things".
"I am searching for a lot with big trees, deciduous trees."
Analyze 360 street-view imagery for the closest trees. Largest appearing trees in the 360 photo.
